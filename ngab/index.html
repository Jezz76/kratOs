GAMEPLAY

In God of War (2018), you play as Kratos, engaging in intense and strategic battles that focus on attacking, dodging, blocking, and parrying. Your son, Atreus, fights alongside you, helping with his bow and special abilities. The game takes place in a stunning Norse-inspired world where you’ll explore, solve puzzles, uncover hidden treasures, and face powerful bosses like gods, dragons, and Valkyries. As you progress, you can upgrade your gear and abilities to grow stronger. The gameplay seamlessly combines action, exploration, and storytelling for an unforgettable adventure.


  • Epic Norse Mythology Adventure: Explore a rich, immersive world inspired by Norse legends.

  • Heartfelt Story: Witness the emotional journey of Kratos and his son, Atreus, as they grow together.

  • Stunning Visuals: Jaw-dropping graphics bring Midgard and other realms to life.
